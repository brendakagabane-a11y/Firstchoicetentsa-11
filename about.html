Our Vision: Quality You Can Trust

First Choice Tents & Aluminium Fabrication was established with a singular mission: to provide Uganda and the East African region with access to highly durable, affordable, and expertly manufactured shelters and structures.

Based in Mukono with a major branch in Arua City, we've spent over a decade building a reputation for meticulous craftsmanship and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction.

We proudly serve organizations, companies, individuals, and international clients, ensuring every product—from a small bar shade to a large-scale aluminium structure—meets the highest international standards.

We are your number one partner for tents, shelters, and aluminium works in Uganda and beyond.

OUR JOURNEY

15 Years of Growth & Innovation

From humble beginnings to becoming Uganda's premier fabrication company.

2008

The Beginning

Founded in Mukono with a vision to provide quality tent solutions to Uganda. Started with a small team of 5 dedicated craftsmen.

2012

Expansion into Aluminium

Diversified services to include aluminium fabrication, becoming one of the first companies in Uganda to offer both specializations.

2016

Arua Branch Opening

Opened our second major facility in Arua City to better serve northern Uganda and expand our nationwide coverage.

2020

500th Project Milestone

Celebrated completing our 500th major project, solidifying our position as Uganda's most trusted fabrication company.

2025

Looking Forward

Continuing to innovate with new technologies, sustainable practices, and expanding our services across East Africa.
